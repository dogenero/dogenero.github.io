	<p>
		After the ground-breaking Bitcoin whitepaper published in 2008 by an anonymous individual or group known as Satoshi Nakamoto,
		the research in the crypto-currencies field exploded.
	</p>
	<p>
		Another anonymous individual known as Nicolas van Saberhagen published the CryptoNote whitepaper in 2013.<br>
		CryptoNote was an open protocol for creating cryptocurrencies which aimed to fix several issues in Bitcoin, such as the lack of privacy.
	</p>
	<p>
		Bytecoin was the first so-called privacy coin, forked from the CryptoNote Protocol.<br>
		However, controversies in the Bytecoin cryptocurrency led to the creation of Monero.
	</p>
