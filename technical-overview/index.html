Supply distribution

Dogenero has an initial distribution of 18 million coins, with block rewards smoothly decreasing to a minumum tail-emission of 1 dogenero per block.
This tail-emission ensures that after the end of classic block reward miners still get rewarded without having to increase transaction fees.

Mining

Dogenero is a CPU-mineable coin. It cannot be mined with ASICs or FPGAs.

Privacy

Dogenero uses several technologies to protect privacy while allowing to verificate transactions.
These techniques include: Confidential Transactions, One-Time Stealth Addresses, and Ring Signatures.

Dogenero has a ring-size of 20 and uses Bulletproofs+ to give good anonimity while keeping transaction size small.
